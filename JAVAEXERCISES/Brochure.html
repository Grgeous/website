Beautiful Spots in Oriental Mindoro

Puerto Galera

Puerto Galera is known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. It's a popular destination for snorkeling, diving, and beach relaxation.

Tamaraw Falls

Tamaraw Falls is a majestic waterfall located in Barangay Villaflor near the town of Puerto Galera. It offers a refreshing escape amidst lush greenery and tranquil surroundings.

Mangyan Village

Experience the rich cultural heritage of Oriental Mindoro by visiting a Mangyan Village. Interact with the indigenous Mangyan people, learn about their traditions, and admire their handicrafts.
