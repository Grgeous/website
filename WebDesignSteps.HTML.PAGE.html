Web Design Steps

  • Determine the Intended Audience
    The colors, imagegs, fonts, and layout should be tailored to the prefenrences of your audience. The type of site content (reading level, amount of animation, etc.) should be appropriate for your chosen audience.
  • Determine the Goals of the Site
    Some common goals of web sites are: to be informative, to create a personal home page, to establish a sorporate web presence, and to do business in e-commerce.
  • Determine the General Content and Create a Site Map
    The site map is sometimes called a Storyboard. Common organizational structures for Web sites:hierarchical, linear, and random.
  • Sketch a Tentative Page Layout
    Be consistent in your overall layout --hr color, typefaces, logo location, navigation bar.etc.Plan your Home Page Layout To avoid scrolling. Plan your Content Page Layout to be consistent, with easy navigation.
